Dear reader, I have decided to keep Saturdays as my standard blog day. Sometimes when a lot happens I will post more than once a week as I did this week. There are two things that I would like to talk about especially in this blog, which is work and work. As one might know I currently have two jobs, mentorship at the university and my tutoring company. I’ll discuss both.

I would like to start by saying mentorship is not that hard, however I was let down by my employer. He expects us to work in total for 40 hour for the complete mentorship which translates to 2 hours max a week for a given period of time. This is simply far too low to do proper mentoring. Originally I would work 80 hours. The details aren’t that important, however starting a dialog about the new expected work times is always tiring. As I/we as employees have to defend our case and the employer will defend his case. This takes a strong mental mind to be (1) be sure of your rights (2) the energy to dialog and keep it professional and not personal. This wouldn’t be a problem at all if the guy I was talking to didn’t seem to be such a cold person and my fellow employees would voice their opinion. If we are all in the same boat, it is much easier to argue my case. Well if I have the time this weekend (or starting Monday) I will have to talk about what exactly is in my contract and what are we being paid. Such sensitive topics, which I thought was already clear.

The next experience I had was to let go one of my partners in my tutoring company. He crossed a boundary for the second time. In my mind I have to decide what the consequence should be and how I felt about it. The consequence was to let him go and my other partners agreed. Which let to the way we had to phrase such an conversation. Keeping a healthy personal relationship after having a bad business relationship is new to me. It start with the conversation (basically a firing) and my other partner did the talking, which led to an emotional response of my ex-partner. However the conversation was handled well and I think our personal relationship will be also be fine. One burden dropped at least. I does mean we have to pick-up everything that he left behind. I belief the current partners will lead to a healthy growth and allows our focus once again on the business.

All the talk about business is really tiring. What will next week bring me? FYI, everything felt okay Wednesday because I was starting to get in the flow again now I feel like my back is itching.
