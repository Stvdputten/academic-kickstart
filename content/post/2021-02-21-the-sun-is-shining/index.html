… I feel good and nobody is going to stop me now. Well this Sunday I went on a walk with a friend. Really good weather and everyone should regularly take walks to ease the mind. In contrast to last week the weather feels like spring. I accomplished a surprising amount of work this week. First and most importantly my game Persona 5. I finished watching community season 5 and 6. I got a sort of rhythm going. Finally I expect next week to be my first real week of progress. Let’s talk about it.

After 120 hours of gaming, I finished one of the longest games I ever played — Persona 5. Which means 3 weeks of full time gaming, which is insane given that I have engaged with my thesis full time next to it past 4 week. Well that’s addiction in a nutshell. I would rate the game 9 out of 10, so if you enjoy story and rpgs and reading a lot than go play it!

Community is a sort of cult sitcom show on Netflix. I recently tried it again, to finish the remaining episodes of season 5 and 6. It brought back memories of my previous home. From when I started watching community last year, around the start of corona last year. It bring a nostalgic feeling and that’s why I really like the opening song! Definitely worth a rewatch in the future.

One of my improvements thing week has been about going to the gym again. I went three times. A nice start and in my opinion the least amount of times you should go. If I want to have a nice and fit body I think I should slowly build up to going 5 to 6 times a week. It takes time to get used to such a frequency, but it good for the mind and body. As I noticed, because the after feeling of working out gave me a gentle “high”. At the same time, I do a short but nice HIIT training in the morning to get my body pumped up and active. A few weeks ago when I tried to go to the game I noticed this huge amount of muscle pain afterwards, maybe it’s because of the cold and my bad warming-up routine. Let’s try to keep this up, most people can and so should I.

Next week will be my first serious attempt on getting my coding part of the Thesis going. So let’s see how far I can get. It seems that having a good before bed and morning routine can literally make or break my day. It’s not a must obviously, but I think having one is great. So now starting week 4 of my thesis project, let’s go.

The last thing I want to briefly mention to myself. The better the weather, the more I want to do everything expect work on my thesis. I should do as much as I can right now and following that I can hopefully get a nice summer break! Let’s make most of my time till August!
